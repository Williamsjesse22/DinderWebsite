Why Choose Dinder?

Accommodations and Preferences

Dinder creates a vector anchored down by your accommodations but ever-flexible with your preferences. As you swipe, Dinder learns your tastes and adjusts recommendations accordingly.

Grouping and Friending

Add friends to your Dinder group and share preferences and accommodations across it. When you are in a group, your homepage will update to show restaurants that match the group's preferences and accommodations. You can flip between groups at any time.

Swipe to Decide

When everyone swipes right on the same restaurant, you will match and will be sent information about the restaurant. You may continue Dindering after matching.
