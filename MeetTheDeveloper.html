Jesse Williams

Dinder was created by Jesse Williams, a Software Engineer at Iowa State University. I have a passion for solving problems and my goal is to make the world a better place through technology.

With 9 older siblings, all with varying dietary accommodations and preferences, Googling "food near me" was no longer cutting it. So, I saught out to find a better way to choose a restaurant that takes both your accommodations and prefences seriously and weighs them accordningly. Through the help of many friends and family, the idea for Dinder was born late one night in my freshman dorm room.

With endless support from those around me, this eccentric idea has finally come to reality. Dinder is a culmination of my passion for technology, problem-solving, and the desire to make dining out a more enjoyable experience for everyone. Come with me on this journey to revolutionize the way we choose where to eat through Dinder: the end of dinner debates!
